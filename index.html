***Please check time from 12 am and before sunset for accurate date of Ramadan***

The Sehri and iftar time is based upon the time provided by Islamic Foundation Bangladesh. Note that they've deducted 3 mins from sehri and added 3 mins to iftar time for careful measurement. However the sunnah is to hasting to break the fast after sunset and not to delay it. So break your fast at the provided time after deducting 3 mins IF YOUR TIME IS ACCURATELY UPTO DATE through internet connection.
